The four-metric RAG eval: Faithfulness and Answer relevancy score the generator (does the output match the context, does it answer the question). Contextual relevancy and Contextual recall score the retriever — relevancy is precision (is the chunk on-topic?), recall is coverage (does the chunk contain everything the expected answer needs?). High recall + low faithfulness = generator hallucinated despite good retrieval. Low recall + high faithfulness = retrieval missed key facts and the generator dutifully answered with what little it had.
